About

Who I Am

I grew up in Peru watching institutions fail the people they were supposed to serve. I believe technology can close those gaps, but only if it is built with the people it affects. My goal is to build human-centered tech that is honest about its tradeoffs and designed to expand, not replace, human agency.

Background

Industrial and Systems Engineer from Peru, with an MBA from Politecnico di Milano and an MPS in Information Science from Cornell. I have worked across e-commerce, advertising, and job search platforms as a product manager and analyst.

My work sits at the intersection of technology, human behavior, and systems design.

Project 01 — Ongoing

Elecciones 2026

Problem

In Peru, corruption is normalized. The JNE (national electoral body) makes candidate info public, but the website is dense and hard to navigate. Voters struggle to compare candidates on policy, background, or track record.

Goal: Make information easy to scan without nudging voters toward any candidate.

Built With

Claude Code Web Scraping JNE Data
Project 01 — Approach

Elecciones 2026

Approach

Restructured the information architecture to surface policy positions, background, and track record in a scannable, comparable format.

"The hardest design decision was not layout: it was deciding what to show and how much. Every choice about what to surface or suppress is implicitly an editorial decision. Neutrality is not a default. It has to be actively designed for."

Solution

02

Project 02 — Process & Results

ApplAI

Process

User interviews, heuristic evaluation, and usability testing across multi-screen workflows.

"The most valuable intervention was making systemic information legible so candidates could make informed choices from the start."

Key Finding

The companies' hiring practices view resonated most strongly. Users wanted visibility before applying — not just a complaint button after something went wrong.

Project 03 — PM Internship

Jobrapido

Problem

Low retention. Users were not coming back.

"The UX was the bottleneck. If we made the search experience meaningfully better, retention would follow."

Three Levers

Filtering

Built ML keyword extraction workflow to improve job-to-query matching (80% accuracy)

UI Redesign

Restructured results page with inline job preview and rebalanced visual hierarchy

Subscription

Reworked copy and flow to convert more users into returning ones

Project 03 — Results

Jobrapido

Data-driven redesign proposal with measurable improvements tracked across engagement metrics using an A/B testing framework with Looker dashboards.

Technical Achievement

Used Fireworks AI to test and compare models. Fine-tuned a zero-shot prompt with Mistral until reaching 80% keyword extraction accuracy.
